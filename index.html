Welcome to PBL

PRINCIPLE BASED LEADERSHIP FAMILY (PBL) is a community-based charity in Bungoma County, Kenya. It promotes sustainable societal transformation through male engagement, education, health programs, and empowerment training, partnering with Kenyan Prisons Services for inmate rehabilitation and reducing recidivism.

About Us

Empowerment and Sustainable Livelihoods

PBL promotes sustainable livelihoods by offering entrepreneurial training, skills development, and sustainable farming initiatives. These programs empower individuals to achieve financial independence and contribute to long-term community growth.

Learn More

Community Health and Well-being Support

PBL enhances community health by offering healthcare programs, promoting preventive care, and improving access to medical services. We focus on health awareness, mental health, maternal care, and nutrition to support overall well-being in the region.

Learn More

Education Support

PBL supports education through scholarships and learning materials, along with the development of school infrastructure and facilities. These initiatives have increased access to education and enhanced its quality in the region.

Our Vision and Mission

We are committed to aligning our operations with the mission and vision of the organization.

Vision

Our vision is to be an accomplished agent of societal sustainable development and transformative agenda..

Mission

Our mission is to drive societal change through male engagement, education, health programs, organic farming, and entrepreneurial empowerment. We also partner with Kenyan Prisons Services for inmate training and reducing recidivism.

Our Core Value

Embrace Diversity

Foster an environment that celebrates and values differences in culture, background, and perspective. We believe that diversity enriches our community, enhances creativity, and promotes mutual understanding and respect among individuals from all walks of life.

Honesty, Integrity, and Probity

Uphold the highest standards of truthfulness, transparency, and ethical conduct. We are committed to maintaining integrity in every action and decision, building trust with stakeholders, and setting an example of moral leadership and responsibility.

Teamwork

Encourage collaboration and collective effort, recognizing that our strength lies in unity. By working together and leveraging each individual's strengths, we can achieve common goals and overcome challenges more effectively than through individual effort alone.

Observe Personal Respect and Dignity

Treat all individuals with respect, valuing their inherent worth and rights. We are dedicated to creating an atmosphere where everyone feels valued, heard, and empowered, ensuring that personal dignity and human rights are upheld at all times.

Efficient and Effective Hard Work

Commit to excellence through diligent effort and smart use of resources. We aim to maximize productivity and achieve impactful results, maintaining a focus on quality, innovation, and continuous improvement in all our programs and services.

Accountability

Take full responsibility for our actions, decisions, and commitments. We foster a culture of transparency and reliability, where individuals and teams are answerable to stakeholders, ensuring that promises are kept, and expectations are met.
